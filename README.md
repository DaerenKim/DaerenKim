## About Me

I'm a penultimate-year Data Science & Analytics student at the National University of Singapore, pursuing a second major in Quantitative Finance and a minor in Artificial Intelligence.

My work sits at the intersection of Data Science, Machine Learning, and AI Engineering. I enjoy building end-to-end systems that transform messy, real-world data into scalable products and actionable insights.

Recently, I've been working on:
- Agentic AI systems and workflow automation
- Advanced Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- OCR and document intelligence pipelines
- Predictive modelling and analytics
- Large-scale data engineering and dashboarding

I'm particularly interested in developing practical AI solutions for finance, insurance and enterprise operations.
